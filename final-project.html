Gragon Ball Power Sensor

Greetings! Our final project for interactive web class is based on the ki/chi mechanism in the popular anime Dragon Ball. You can learn it here

Game Mechanism

By contracting muscle and reading in the contraction with Myo muscle sensor the VR world will generate yellow orbs/particles based on the level of contraction.
We will add more particle or environmental elements into the world as we progress.
